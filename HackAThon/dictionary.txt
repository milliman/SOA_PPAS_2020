# Attributes for training and testing datasets:
1 studymanual - the study manual that student is using ("A" or "B")
2 reason - reason to choose this study manual (nominal: close to "home", school "reputation", "course" preference or "other")
3 age - student's age (numeric: from 18 to 25)
4 Medu - mother's education (numeric: 0 - none,  1 - primary education (4th grade), 2 – 5th to 9th grade, 3 – secondary education or 4 – higher education)
5 Fedu - father's education (numeric: 0 - none,  1 - primary education (4th grade), 2 – 5th to 9th grade, 3 – secondary education or 4 – higher education)
6 Mjob - mother's job (nominal: "teacher", "health" care related, civil "services" (e.g. administrative or police), "at_home" or "other")
7 studytime - weekly study time (numeric: 1 - <5 hours, 2 - 5 to 10 hours, 3 - 10 to 15 hours, or 4 - >15 hours)
8 failures - number of past exam failures (numeric: n if 1<=n<3, else 4)
9 goout - going out with friends (numeric: from 1 - very low to 5 - very high)
10 Dalc - workday alcohol consumption (numeric: from 1 - very low to 5 - very high)
11 Walc - weekend alcohol consumption (numeric: from 1 - very low to 5 - very high)
12 health - current health status (numeric: from 1 - very bad to 5 - very good)
13 studydays - percentage of study days taken (numeric: from 0 to 1)
14 higher - wants to take higher education (binary: yes or no)
15 G1 - grade on practice exam 1 (numeric: from 0 to 10)
16 G2 - grade on practice exam 2 (numeric: from 0 to 10)
17 result - final result (binary: pass or fail, target variable)
